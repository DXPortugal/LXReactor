What is the Journey to the Web Summit Program?

The Journey to Web Summit Program is a Microsoft Program designed to support and select the best startups in Portugal and showcase them at the Microsoft booth during the Web Summit conference that will take place in Lisbon later this year.

Who can participate in the Startup Challenge?

Startups based in Portugal who meet the following criteria:

  • being a member of the Microsoft BizSpark Program .To become a member of the BizSpark Program, at no cost, click here
  • having a product or solution (or working on a Microsoft technology version of a product or solution) that can be showcased at the Microsoft booth during Web Summit

Note that using Microsoft technology is not a requirement to participate but all the proposed solutions will be evaluated according to the potential and/or the interest in adopting Microsoft technologies.

Who will select the winner?

Microsoft and a set of partners will evaluate all applications and select 8 finalists. On the final pitch, the 8 remaining startups will be presenting to a jury comprised of international venture capitalists, startup accelerators and Microsoft technical advisors who will select the winner.

How can I participate?

To apply for the startup challenge, you need to fill out this form, where you will be asked to provide some information about your startup, including a link to a brief pitch deck or video that describes your product or solution, and how you are going to leverage Microsoft technology. You can apply until October 7th.

Remember, to apply for the challenge you need to be a member of the BizSpark Program. If you are not, you should register in this link and you will get an acceptance e-mail confirmation if you meet the program requirements. You can check the BizSpark Program eligibility criteria here.

Ok, I’ve done all the steps and am registered for the Journey to the Web Summit Program. What’s next?

After receiving all applications, a local jury will select 8 finalists by October 12th. Regardless of wether you are among the finalists you will receive an e-mail communication letting you know the result of your application. If you are among the finalists, your e-mail will contain everything you need to know to proceed to the final pitch.

Throughout September and October, our teams and partners will also be delivering a set of open online masterclasses that you can attend to help you prepare and make the most out of Web Summit, with topics ranging from Growth Hacking, Investment 101 to more technical sessions on IoT, Bots and Machine Learning. You can see the full list of upcoming sessions and watch them live.
